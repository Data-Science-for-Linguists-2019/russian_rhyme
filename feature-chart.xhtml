Based on http://linguistics.byu.edu/faculty/eddingtond/223/distinctive%20features%20chart.pdf. Syllabic and consonantal are mutually exclusive, so the latter is omitted. For consonants, position is fully defined by anterior and coronal, so back, high, and low are omitted. Round and strident are omitted because they are not distinctive. Palatalized has been added (includes true palatals). For vowels, position is fully defined by high, low, and back; round and ATR are omitted. Values have been supplied for all features, even when they are not actually distinctive for particular segments (e.g., all fricatives have been classified as –delayed release).
